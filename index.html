Introduction

What is HERE?

HERE is a miniprogram that will let you explore the world and discover new places, without ever having to leave WeChat. Find a landmark, open the program and receive a detailed description on the spot

PROBLEM

Lugging a heavy, outdated guidebook around or browsing through endless Google search results

"I love bringing 5kg of guides when traveling and spending 20 minutes scanning shitty ad-ridden websites!"
-Nobody ever

TARGET

Domestic and international (Chinese) travelers

"By 2030 China will account for a quarter of international tourism"
-UNWTO

SOLUTION

A WeChat miniprogram that calculates your location and instantly returns detailed information of the tourist attraction you're at.

"Take heart: the future of travel is lightweight and increasingly eco-friendly, with tips and itineraries beamed straight to your smartphone."
-Lonely Planet
